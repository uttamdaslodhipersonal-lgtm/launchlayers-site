FAQ

Common Questions,
Honest Answers.

How long does a website take to build?
+
Most projects go live within 7–14 days from the moment we receive everything we need from you. More complex builds may take a little longer — we'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do I need to provide the content?
+
We'll guide you on exactly what's needed — logo, images, and any key text. If you need help with copy, just let us know and we can assist.
Will my website work on mobile?
+
Every site we build is mobile-first by default. It will look and perform great on phones, tablets, and desktops.
How many revisions do I get?
+
Every project includes 2 rounds of revisions. We work closely with you throughout the process so by the time we reach review, we're already aligned on the direction.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
+
Yes — we offer post-launch support packages for updates, edits, and technical maintenance. Just ask and we'll put together a plan that suits your needs.
What do you need from me to get started?
+
Just fill out the project brief using the form on this page. We'll review it and get back to you within 24 hours to discuss next steps.
Can you redesign my existing website?
+
Absolutely — redesigns are one of our most common projects. Whether your site looks outdated or just isn't converting, we can rebuild it from the ground up.
How does payment work?
+
We require a 20% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon delivery. We accept payments via bank transfer and major online payment methods.
